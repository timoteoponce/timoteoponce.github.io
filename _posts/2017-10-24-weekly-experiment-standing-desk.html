--- layout: post title: 'Weekly Experiment: Standing desk' date: '2017-10-24T11:24:00.001-07:00' author: Timoteo Ponce tags: - timebox - experiment modified_time: '2017-10-24T11:24:22.096-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5502681789285906166.post-1877998185476947013 blogger_orig_url: https://timoponce.blogspot.com/2017/10/weekly-experiment-standing-desk.html --- After listening to some interesting thoughts from Linda Rising at Software Engineering Radio, I've started a periodical experiment, time-boxed to a week in order to check results.

So this weeks is related to standing desk, not really a standing desk, but a platform that allows me to work while standing up.

The rules:
  • During mornings sitting will be the default 
  • During afternoons standing will be the default
  • During lunch-time standing will be the default
The control:
  • Did it have any physical effect? 
  • Did it have any food/drink related effect?
  • Did it have any sleep-related effect?
  • Did it affect in any way productivity and awareness?
