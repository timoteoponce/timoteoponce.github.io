--- layout: post title: Configure DiffMerge on MacOSX date: '2011-03-23T07:13:00.000-07:00' author: Timoteo Ponce tags: - git - diff - mac modified_time: '2011-07-15T11:17:38.669-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5502681789285906166.post-3820515119444558925 blogger_orig_url: https://timoponce.blogspot.com/2011/03/configure-diffmerge-on-macosx.html --- Based on this article, I modified it a little bit in order to make it work on Mac:

[diff]   tool=diffmerge                                                                                                                   
[difftool "diffmerge"]                                                                                                                      
  cmd = exec /Applications/DiffMerge.app/Contents/MacOS/DiffMerge \"$LOCAL\" \"$REMOTE\"                                                                                                                                   
[merge]                                                                                                                                     
  tool = diffmerge                                                                                                                    
[mergetool "diffmerge"]                                                                                                                            
  cmd = exec /Applications/DiffMerge.app/Contents/MacOS/DiffMerge --merge --result=\"$MERGED\"\n\"$LOCAL\" \"$BASE\" \"$REMOTE\"                                                                                           
  trustexitcode = false
